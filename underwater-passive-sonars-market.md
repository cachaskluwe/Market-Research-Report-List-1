<p><strong>The Underwater Passive Sonars Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Frequency</li><li>Medium Frequency</li><li>High Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Underwater passive sonars are categorized into three market types based on frequency: low, medium, and high. Low-frequency sonars (below 1 kHz) excel in long-range detection, penetrating deeper waters but offering lower resolution. Medium-frequency sonars (1 kHz to 10 kHz) provide a balance of range and resolution, suitable for tracking large vessels. High-frequency sonars (above 10 kHz) deliver high-resolution images for close-range detection, ideal for identifying smaller objects, but have limited range and are affected by environmental noise.</p></p>

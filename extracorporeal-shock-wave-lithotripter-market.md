<p><p>The Extracorporeal Shock Wave Lithotripter (ESWL) market is categorized into three main types: electrohydraulic, electromagnetic, and piezoelectric. </p><p>Electrohydraulic lithotripters generate shock waves through electrical discharges in water, providing effective stone fragmentation. Electromagnetic devices use electromagnetic coils to produce shock waves, offering a more controlled energy delivery. Piezoelectric lithotripters utilize piezoelectric crystals to create shock waves, known for their compact design and efficient performance. Each type caters to different patient needs and clinical setups, influencing the market dynamics.</p></p>

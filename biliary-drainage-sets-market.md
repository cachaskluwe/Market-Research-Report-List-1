<p><p>The biliary drainage sets market comprises two main types: biliary drainage catheters and biliary drainage bags. Biliary drainage catheters are devices used to relieve obstructions in the bile duct, allowing bile to flow from the liver into the intestine, which helps prevent complications such as jaundice or infection. Biliary drainage bags, on the other hand, collect the drained bile externally, managing excess leakage while maintaining hygiene. Together, they provide essential solutions in gastrointestinal and hepatobiliary care.</p></p>

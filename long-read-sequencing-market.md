<p><strong>The Long Read Sequencing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Molecule Real-Time Sequencing (SMRT)</li><li>Nanopore Sequencing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The long-read sequencing market primarily includes two key technologies: Single-Molecule Real-Time (SMRT) sequencing and Nanopore sequencing. SMRT sequencing allows for real-time observation of DNA synthesis, offering high accuracy and the ability to sequence through complex regions. In contrast, Nanopore sequencing utilizes protein nanopores to detect changes in ionic current as nucleotides pass through, enabling rapid sequencing of long DNA fragments. Both technologies address the need for comprehensive genomic analysis, facilitating advancements in personalized medicine and genomics research.</p></p>

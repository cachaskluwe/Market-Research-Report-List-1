<p><strong>The Automotive Wet Friction Clutch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Plate Clutch</li><li>Multi Plate Clutch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive wet friction clutch market comprises primarily two types: single plate clutches and multi-plate clutches. Single plate clutches feature a single friction disc and are commonly used in smaller vehicles due to their simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Multi-plate clutches, on the other hand, consist of multiple friction discs stacked together, allowing for greater torque transmission and better performance, making them ideal for high-power applications like racing or heavy-duty vehicles. Both types rely on lubrication, enhancing durability and efficiency.</p></p>

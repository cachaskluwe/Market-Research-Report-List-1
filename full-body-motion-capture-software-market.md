<p><p>The full body motion capture software market is divided into three primary types: optical, inertia, and others. Optical systems use cameras to track reflective markers placed on a subject, capturing precise motion in 3D space. Inertia-based systems rely on sensors that measure acceleration and rotation, allowing for mobility without external cameras. The "others" category includes hybrid systems and advanced technologies like depth-sensing cameras, catering to diverse applications in gaming, film, virtual reality, and biomechanics research.</p></p>

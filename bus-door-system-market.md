<p><p>The bus door system market is primarily divided into two types: electrical actuator systems and pneumatic actuator systems. Electrical actuator systems utilize electric motors to control the opening and closing of doors, offering precise operation and reduced maintenance costs. In contrast, pneumatic actuator systems rely on compressed air to function, providing robust performance in various environmental conditions but requiring more maintenance due to air supply needs. Both systems are essential for ensuring passenger safety and comfort in public transportation.</p></p>

<p><p>The semiconductor ceramic target market is primarily divided into two types: planar targets and rotary targets. Planar targets are flat and used in conventional sputtering processes, offering uniform deposition for various applications. In contrast, rotary targets are cylindrical and allow for continuous material usage, enhancing efficiency and throughput. Each type serves distinct needs in semiconductor manufacturing, with planar targets being ideal for small-scale operations and rotary targets suited for high-volume production environments, driving advancements in device fabrication technologies.</p></p>

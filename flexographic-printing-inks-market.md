<p><p>The flexographic printing inks market is categorized into several types based on their composition. Water-based inks are eco-friendly, offering low VOC emissions and quick drying times, making them suitable for food packaging and paper products. Solvent-based inks, known for their strong adhesion and vibrant colors, are commonly used in various substrates but have higher VOC levels. UV-cured inks utilize ultraviolet light to rapidly cure the ink, providing durability and high gloss. Other inks may include hybrid formulations or specialty inks tailored for specific applications.</p></p>

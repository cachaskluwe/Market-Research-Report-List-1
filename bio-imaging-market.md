<p><p>The bio-imaging market encompasses various imaging modalities used for medical diagnostics. X-ray radiography employs X-ray radiation to visualize internal structures, particularly bones and dense tissues. Medical ultrasonography, or ultrasound, uses high-frequency sound waves to create real-time images of soft tissues and organs. Magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) utilizes magnetic fields and radio waves to generate detailed images of internal body structures. Elastography measures tissue stiffness, enhancing diagnosis of conditions like liver fibrosis. Other market types may include computed tomography (CT) and positron emission tomography (PET).</p></p>

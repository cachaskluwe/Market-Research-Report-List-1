<p><p>The Four-Stroke Engine Oil market is divided into two main types: air-cooled oil and liquid-cooled oil. Air-cooled oil is specifically formulated for engines that dissipate heat through air, providing enhanced viscosity and stability at high temperatures. Liquid-cooled oil, on the other hand, is designed for engines that use a coolant system to manage temperature, offering superior protection against wear and thermal breakdown. Each type is tailored to meet the specific requirements and operating conditions of different engine designs.</p></p>

<p><p>An RF choke is a passive electronic component designed to impede the flow of radio frequency (RF) signals while allowing direct current (DC) or low-frequency signals to pass through. It plays a crucial role in various applications, including RF amplifiers, filters, and power supplies, ensuring that high-frequency noise does not disrupt circuit performance. </p><p>The RF choke market is experiencing significant growth, propelled by increasing demand for wireless communication technologies, such as 5G and IoT devices.

<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The VSD Occluder Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Membranous VSD Occluder</li><li>Muscular VSD Occluder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VSD occluder device market comprises two primary types: membranous VSD occluders and muscular VSD occluders. Membranous VSD occluders are designed for defects located in the membranous septum of the heart, utilizing a mesh-like material to effectively close the opening. In contrast, muscular VSD occluders target defects in the muscular portion of the septum, employing a flexible mechanism that adapts to the surrounding heart tissue. Both types aim to reduce the risks associated with ventricular septal defects efficiently and minimally invasively.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Single-stage Magnetic Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary Shaft</li><li>Rotating Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-stage magnetic pump market is characterized by two main types: stationary shaft and rotating shaft designs. Stationary shaft magnetic pumps feature a fixed drive magnet that allows fluid transport through magnetic coupling without contact, enhancing efficiency and reducing wear. In contrast, rotating shaft magnetic pumps utilize a rotating magnet to drive the fluid, providing high flow rates and adaptability for various applications. Both types leverage magnetic technology to eliminate mechanical seals, minimizing leakage and maintenance in fluid handling systems.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Desiccant Dryer</li><li>Refrigerated dryer</li><li>Membrane dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air dryer market comprises three main types: desiccant dryers, refrigerated dryers, and membrane dryers. Desiccant dryers use moisture-absorbing materials to remove humidity from compressed air, ideal for applications requiring low humidity levels. Refrigerated dryers cool compressed air to condense moisture, suitable for moderate drying needs. Membrane dryers utilize selective permeation through membranes to separate moisture from air, offering a compact solution for low to medium moisture removal. Each type caters to specific industrial requirements for efficient air drying.</p></p>

<p><p>The residential PV inverter market primarily consists of three types: string inverters, micro inverters, and other inverter solutions. String inverters connect multiple solar panels in series, offering cost efficiency and simplicity. Micro inverters, on the other hand, are installed on each panel, enhancing performance in shaded conditions and providing individual panel monitoring. Other market solutions include power optimizers and hybrid inverters, which combine features of string inverters and battery storage, catering to diverse consumer needs and system configurations.</p></p>

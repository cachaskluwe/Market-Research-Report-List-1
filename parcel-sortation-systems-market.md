<p><p>The parcel sortation systems market features different types, primarily horizontal and vertical cross strip sorters. Horizontal cross strip sorters transport parcels along a flat surface, efficiently directing items to multiple output lanes using strategically placed strips. In contrast, vertical cross strip sorters operate in an upright orientation, enabling items to be sorted vertically and optimizing space. Both systems enhance logistical efficiency and speed in sorting operations, catering to various industries, including e-commerce and logistics, to meet increasing parcel volumes.</p></p>

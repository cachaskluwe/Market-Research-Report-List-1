<p><p>Raiseboring equipment is categorized into three main types: impact drills, rotary drills, and vibration drills. Impact drills utilize high-energy impacts to break rock, making them suitable for hard formations. Rotary drills use a rotating bit to cut through material, providing efficiency in various rock types and depths. Vibration drills employ high-frequency vibrations to facilitate penetration, minimizing resistance and improving speed. Each type serves distinct applications in mining and civil engineering, catering to different geological conditions and project requirements.</p></p>

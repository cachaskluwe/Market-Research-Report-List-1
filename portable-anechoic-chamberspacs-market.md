<p><p>Portable Anechoic Chambers (PACs) are specialized soundproof environments designed for accurate acoustic measurements. The market includes two main types: Free Sound Field Space and Semi-Free Sound Field Space. Free Sound Field Space provides full isolation from external noise and reflections, ideal for pure sound testing. In contrast, Semi-Free Sound Field Space allows limited sound interactions for applications like product testing under specific conditions. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, aerospace, and consumer electronics, enhancing sound quality evaluation.</p></p>

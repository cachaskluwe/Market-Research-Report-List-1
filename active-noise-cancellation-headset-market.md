<p><p>The Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) headset market is categorized into three types: full cup, on-ear, and in-ear. Full cup headsets, also known as over-ear, envelop the ears completely, providing maximum noise isolation and comfort for extended use. On-ear headsets rest on the ears, offering a balance between portability and effective noise cancellation. In-ear headsets fit snugly in the ear canal, delivering a compact design with decent noise reduction and enhanced sound quality, making them ideal for active lifestyles.</p></p>

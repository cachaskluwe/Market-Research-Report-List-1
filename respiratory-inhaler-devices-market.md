<p><p>The respiratory inhaler devices market comprises three main types: Dry Powder Inhalers (DPIs), Metered Dose Inhalers (MDIs), and nebulizers. DPIs deliver medication in powdered form, requiring the patient to inhale forcefully for effective dosage. MDIs use a propellant to deliver a mist of medication with each actuation, facilitating precise dosing. Nebulizers transform liquid medication into a fine aerosol, ideal for patients who may struggle with inhalation techniques. Each device caters to different needs in respiratory therapy, enhancing medication delivery and patient compliance.</p></p>

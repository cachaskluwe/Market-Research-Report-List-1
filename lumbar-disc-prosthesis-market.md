<p><strong>The Lumbar Disc Prosthesis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metal-on-metal</li><li>Metal-on-biopolymer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lumbar disc prosthesis market is primarily categorized into two types: metal-on-metal and metal-on-biopolymer. Metal-on-metal prostheses utilize durable metal components that provide stability and long-lasting performance, making them suitable for younger, active patients. In contrast, metal-on-biopolymer prostheses combine metal with a biopolymer material, offering enhanced flexibility and reduced wear. This hybrid approach aims to mimic natural disc movements while minimizing friction, catering to patients seeking a more biocompatible and less invasive solution for disc-related spinal issues.</p></p>

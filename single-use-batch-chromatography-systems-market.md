<p><p>Single-use batch chromatography systems are categorized into three main market types based on flow rates. Laboratory-grade systems handle flow rates between 0-500 mL/min, ideal for research and small-scale applications. Pilot-grade systems, operating at 500-1000 mL/min, are designed for scaling up processes and validating production methods. Production-grade systems, exceeding 1 L/min, cater to large-scale manufacturing, offering efficiency and flexibility in bioprocessing. Each category serves specific needs, ensuring optimal performance and reduced contamination risks.</p></p>

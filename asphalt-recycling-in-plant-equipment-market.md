<p><strong>The Asphalt Recycling In-plant Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Mix Regeneration</li><li>Cold Mix Regeneration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Asphalt recycling in-plant equipment encompasses technologies that allow the reuse of reclaimed asphalt pavement (RAP). The Hot Mix Regeneration process involves heating RAP to high temperatures, blending it with new asphalt and aggregates to create high-quality hot mix asphalt. In contrast, Cold Mix Regeneration utilizes emulsified asphalt to blend with RAP at ambient temperatures, making it suitable for less demanding applications or locations where heating is impractical. Both methods enhance sustainability while reducing material costs in road construction.</p></p>

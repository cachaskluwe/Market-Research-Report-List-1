<p><p>The Parkinson's Disease drug market includes various types of medications aimed at managing symptoms. Sinemet-CR is a combination of levodopa and carbidopa, providing extended relief from motor symptoms. Trastal, also known as trihexyphenidyl, is used primarily for muscle stiffness and tremors. Madopar combines levodopa with benserazide for better absorption. COMT inhibitors, like entacapone, enhance the effectiveness of levodopa by preventing its breakdown. Other medications may include agonists and adjunct therapies to further address the diverse needs of patients.</p></p>

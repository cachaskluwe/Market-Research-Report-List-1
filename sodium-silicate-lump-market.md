<p><p>The Sodium Silicate Lump market is segmented into three primary types based on molar ratio: Medium Mol, High Mol, and Extra-High Mol. Medium Mol sodium silicate typically has a molar ratio of 2.0 to 3.2 and is used in detergents and adhesives. High Mol, with a ratio of 3.2 to 4.0, finds applications in ceramics and sealants. Extra-High Mol, exceeding a ratio of 4.0, is utilized in specialized industrial processes, such as in the manufacturing of silica products.</p></p>

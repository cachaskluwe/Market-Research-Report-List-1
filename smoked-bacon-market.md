<p><p>The smoked bacon market is divided into two primary types: hot smoked bacon and cold smoked bacon. Hot smoked bacon is cured and then smoked at high temperatures, resulting in a fully cooked product with a rich, smoky flavor, making it ready for immediate consumption. In contrast, cold smoked bacon is cured first and then smoked at lower temperatures, allowing for a more delicate flavor profile and a texture that requires further cooking before consumption. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences and culinary uses.</p></p>

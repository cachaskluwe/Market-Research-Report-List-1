<p><strong>The Cardiac Implantable Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pacemakers</li><li>Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillator (ICD)</li><li>Cardiac Loop Recorders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiac implantable device market features several key types. Pacemakers regulate heart rhythms by delivering electrical impulses to manage arrhythmias. Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs) monitor heartbeats and can deliver shocks to correct life-threatening arrhythmias. Cardiac Loop Recorders are used for long-term continuous monitoring of heart rhythms, capturing episodes that may not occur frequently. These devices enhance patient outcomes by providing essential data for diagnosing and treating various cardiac conditions, ultimately improving quality of life.</p></p>

<p><p>The Radiology Microcatheter System market includes various types tailored for specific applications. Diagnostic microcatheters are used for precise imaging and diagnosis by facilitating contrast delivery. Delivery microcatheters enable targeted therapy by delivering medications or devices directly to a site. Steerable microcatheters offer enhanced maneuverability, allowing for complex navigation within vascular systems. Aspiration microcatheters are designed to remove unwanted materials, such as thrombus, during interventions. Each type plays a crucial role in improving patient outcomes in minimally invasive procedures.</p></p>

<p><p>The lubricant base oil market comprises two primary types: mineral oil and synthetic oil. Mineral oils, derived from refining crude oil, are widely used due to their cost-effectiveness and availability. They offer sufficient lubrication for various applications but may have limitations in performance at extreme temperatures. Synthetic oils, on the other hand, are chemically formulated to provide superior performance, enhanced stability, and better resistance to oxidation and thermal breakdown, making them ideal for demanding applications in automotive and industrial sectors.</p></p>

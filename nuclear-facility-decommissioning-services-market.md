Boiling Water Reactor (BWR) decommissioning focuses on handling the boiling and steam processes, requiring different decontamination techniques. The "Others" category includes diverse reactor types and facilities, each presenting unique challenges and regulatory requirements, necessitating tailored decommissioning approaches to ensure safety and compliance.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Photoresist Stripping Solution Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist Stripper</li><li>Negative Photoresist Stripper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photoresist stripping solution market is primarily divided into two types: positive and negative photoresist strippers. Positive photoresist strippers are designed to dissolve light-exposed areas of the photoresist film, facilitating the removal of patterns on substrates, commonly used in semiconductor manufacturing. Conversely, negative photoresist strippers target unexposed areas, effectively removing the regions where exposure did not occur. Both types are essential for achieving precision in photolithography, ensuring clean surfaces for further processing in the electronics and microfabrication industries.</p></p>

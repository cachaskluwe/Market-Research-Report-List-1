<p><p>The aviation glasses market comprises two primary types: single layer and interlayer glasses. Single layer glasses typically consist of a single piece of transparent material, offering basic protection and visibility for pilots. In contrast, interlayer glasses incorporate multiple layers, often with specialized coatings that provide enhanced durability, UV protection, and anti-reflective properties. These multilayer options are designed to withstand harsher conditions and improve visibility, making them more suitable for commercial and military aviation applications where safety and performance are critical.</p></p>

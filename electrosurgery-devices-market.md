<p><p>The electrosurgery devices market includes three main types: electrosurgery generators, electrosurgery instruments, and accessories. Electrosurgery generators produce high-frequency electrical currents that enable precise tissue cutting and coagulation. Electrosurgery instruments, such as diathermy pens and forceps, are specialized tools that physically apply the generated energy to the target tissue. Accessories enhance the functionality and safety of these devices, including grounding pads, cables, and electrode tips. Together, they play a crucial role in minimally invasive surgical procedures across various medical fields.</p></p>

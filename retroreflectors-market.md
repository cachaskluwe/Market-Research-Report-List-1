<p><p>The retroreflector market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Single Mode Retroreflectors** are designed for applications requiring precise signal transmission over long distances, commonly used in telecommunications and sensing. </p><p>2. **Polarization Maintaining Retroreflectors** preserve the polarization of incoming light, crucial for advanced optical systems and fiber optics. </p><p>3. **Multimode Retroreflectors** support multiple light modes, making them suitable for various applications, including automotive and industrial uses, where flexibility and broad-bandwidth reflection are essential.</p></p>

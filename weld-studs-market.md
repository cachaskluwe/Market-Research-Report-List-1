<p><p>The weld studs market can be categorized into three primary types: manual welding, arc welding, and energy storage welding. Manual welding involves skilled operators using handheld tools for precise placement and joining, offering flexibility for various applications. Arc welding utilizes electric arcs to generate heat, ensuring strong and consistent bonds, making it suitable for industrial use. Energy storage welding leverages stored electrical energy to rapidly weld studs, enhancing efficiency and minimizing heat distortion, ideal for mass production and automated environments.</p></p>

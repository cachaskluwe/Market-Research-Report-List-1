<p><ul><li>Natural Mullite</li><li>Fused Mullite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mullite market is primarily divided into two types: natural mullite and fused mullite. Natural mullite is derived from bauxite and kaolin, characterized by its high stability and thermal resistance, making it suitable for refractory applications. Fused mullite, produced through melting alumina and silica, offers enhanced mechanical strength and thermal shock resistance, ideal for high-temperature environments. Both types are essential in industries such as ceramics, metallurgy, and construction, serving various applications where durability and heat resistance are crucial.</p></p>
